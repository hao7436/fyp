Helping seniors overcome technological barriers and scam propaganda

There are two well-known trends in Singapore. One, the country is in the midst of transforming itself into a Smart Nation, harnessing technology to improve the standard of living. Two, its population is ageing, with life expectancy growing and birth rates remaining low. These factors have given rise to worries about the digital divide, where seniors become unable to navigate a world where technology is increasingly prominent. Even as we digitalise and enjoy the benefits of tech, commentators have pointed out that it is important to ensure that those who are unfamiliar with tech are not left behind.

https://www.tech.gov.sg/media/technews/bridging-the-digital-divide

The purpose of this event is to help older adults overcome technological barriers and help them recognize, learn and use smart devices. The more they use these devices, the more likely they are to be scammed, so while we teach them how to use it, we also need to educate them about how to prevent scams. Improve your awareness of prevention, and at the same time use equipment to improve the quality of life, in order to enjoy the convenience brought by technology.

what are we going to do:

lecture about common functions of smart devices

Introduction and usage of common apps

Lectures on phone scams and SMS scams to raise awareness among the elderly

One-to-one explanation
