ONLINE ANTI-SCAM EDUCATIONAL TALK

This activity is supported by the Singapore Police Force. We will invite an experienced anti-fraud lecturer to explain the most common fraud methods. I believe you will gain something from the lecture and stay away from scams. We also welcome friends who have relevant experience to share with us and work together to fight fraud.
